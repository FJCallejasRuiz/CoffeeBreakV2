Released in 29 ENE 2020. "Coffee Talk" is a game that invites you to be a barista in a mystical night-time cafe in Seattle, serving a variety of characters from both the modern world and mythical folklore. Through meaningful conversations and careful decisions, you develop relationships and explore deep narratives in a world where magic is commonplace and orcs, elves, and mermaids walk among us. -IGN

"In Coffee Talk, we've distilled our passion and creativity to deliver an emotionally resonant, compelling narrative. We're committed to providing a relaxing atmosphere where players can enjoy deep conversations over a cup of tea or coffee. It's a game about listening and understanding, and how something as simple as making a hot beverage can be the bridge to deeper connections and more intriguing stories." - Rosa Álvaro, Founder of Coffee Break Studios.
