¡Conoce al equipo!

"Coffee Break's team is the backbone of our acclaimed video game development. In our Creative Department, art director Sarah Johnson and lead game designer Tomás Rivera blend art and gameplay into captivating experiences. Aisha Patel, our lead writer, and David Kim, scriptwriter, form the core of the Narrative Department, crafting compelling stories that drive our games. In the Technical Department, lead programmer Emily Chen and AI specialist Markus Schmidt transform creative visions into digital realities. The Sound Department, under audio director Michael O'Connor and sound designer Laura García, ensures every game sounds as good as it looks. Guided by marketing expert Sophia Park, our Marketing Department creatively showcases our games to the world. This talented team collaborates seamlessly, making Coffee Break a beacon of innovation in the video game industry."

"Coffee Break, founded in 2010, embodies the spirit of Sevilla's joyous life. It's not just a café; it's a celebration of community and warmth. Each cup of coffee serves as an invitation to savor life's simple pleasures. Amidst Sevilla's vibrant streets, Coffee Break thrives, spreading happiness with every brew. It's a place where every visit becomes a cherished memory, blending the love for coffee with the enchanting soul of Sevilla."

"Coffee Break takes immense pride in their collaboration with renowned voice actors, elevating the artistry of their video games. This partnership reflects a commitment to excellence, ensuring each character resonates with authenticity and depth. The fusion of exceptional voice talent and innovative storytelling brings each game to life, offering players an immersive and emotionally engaging experience. Coffee Break's dedication to such collaborations underscores their passion for creating not just games, but worlds rich in narrative and character."
